Fleet Catalog (if available)

The Fleet Catalog (if available) is a catalog that contains internal part numbers that are used by a specific fleet. These part numbers are equivalent either to genuine or all-makes parts. Consider an example in which the fleet part number for a particular turbocharger is listed as 1234567. This is equivalent to the genuine part number of 85020011, and it is this part that would be purchased from the dealer. The fleet must provide a list of their part numbers together with the corresponding genuine or all makes part numbers.

 

NOTE: For information on how to integrate your fleet catalog into PartsASIST, please contact your local dealer or National Fleet representative.

 

 

To navigate to the Fleet Catalog, click the large Fleet Catalog icon from the home page.

 

 

 

Or, choose Fleet Catalog from the Products menu.

 

 

 

Within the Fleet Catalog, you'll find the same search and filtering capabilities that are available in the Genuine Parts and All Makes catalogs. It's easy to search by the product group icon, or by using the product tab menu list. Or, you can enter a fleet part number in the search bar.

 

 

 

 

After choosing a product category, you'll see the fleet part number shown in a blue hyperlink. The dealer-stocked part number will appear in black immediately below this link. You can check the box and then click the Add to Cart button.

 

As in the other catalogs, you can click on the hyperlink to navigate to the part detail page–on which you can add the part to the cart and checkout.
