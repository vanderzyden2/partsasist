Quick Start - Search from the Product Menu

Another way to search is to begin with a top-level category in the catalog. You can then use filters to narrow the search results until you find the part you need. Throughout this tutorial, you'll find references to other topics that provide more details.

 

The information in the Genuine Parts catalog is structured according to function groups. The main function groups divide into sub-groups that end with a 4-digit function group. By selecting a main group (such as 2 Engine), the sub-groups that correspond to that group will appear in the main window. As you successively choose sub-groups, you'll eventually arrive at the function sub-group that contains the part that you seek.

 

Let's look at an example of how to search for a turbocharger in the Genuine Parts catalog.

 

 

Example: Search for a turbocharger

Follow the steps below:

 

Step 1 – From the menus at the top of the page, choose Products > Genuine Parts.

 

 

Step 2 – Click 2 Engine, engine mounting and equipment.

 

 

 

 

Then, in the main panel, click 25 Intake and exhaust systems, Turbo.

 

 

Click 255 Turbocharger.

 

 

On the 255 Turbocharger page, you'll see all of the turbo chargers that are correspond to every Mack or Volvo truck in your fleet.

 

 

Step 3 – To find the turbocharger for a specific truck, select either a Unit number or VIN to filter the search. Be sure to click the Apply Filter button after selecting either the Unit number or VIN.

 

"FILTER IS APPLIED" will appear above the filter drop-downs, and main panel will display the turbocharger(s) that correspond to the Unit number or VIN.

 

 

 

Step 4 – Click on a turbocharger diagram to display a detail listing all of the parts for that turbocharger. You'll see the part number, description, dealer stock status, and your customer specific pricing.  

 
From here, there are two options:
 

•Option 1 – Click on a blue link in the Part No column to see the detail page for that part. There, you can add the part to your cart by clicking the Add to Cart button.

•Option 2 – By checking the box to the left of the part number and selecting the Add to Cart button, all parts selected will be added to your shopping cart.
