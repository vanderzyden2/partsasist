Quick Start - Search in the Search bar

In this topic, we present a tutorial that shows how to use the search field at the top of the page. You'll learn how to search for a part and filter the results list to find the part you need. Throughout this tutorial, you'll find references to other topics that provide more details.

 

Example: Search for an alternator and filter the results

Follow the steps below:

 

Step 1 – Let's begin with a simple search for alternators.

 

To search for an alternator, you could enter any of the following:

 

•full or partial description, such as "alternator"

•full part number, such as "8600310"

•partial part number, such as "8600"

 

Start by typing "alternator" in the search bar at the top of the page. Then hit Enter or click the small spyglass icon. (For more information, see the Search topic.)

 

 

NOTE: Your results are likely to differ from what appears in the figure, and will correspond to the vehicles in your fleet and the filters you apply.

 

 

 

Step 2 – You can filter the search listing by choosing the catalog in which the part can be found. For example, checking one or more boxes under Genuine Parts will filter the search listing to display parts that are associated with the Genuine Parts catalog. Learn more about this catalog in Genuine Parts.

 

Clearing the filter: Click the Clear Filter button to reset the filtering.

 

 

Step 3 – When you select the Genuine Parts filter, you can narrow the results further by selecting a Unit # as shown in the example below. This will filter the search listing to display items that correspond to that specific Unit. Learn more about adding unit numbers in Manage My Fleet.

 

 

 

Step 4 – Click on a link in the results listing to view detail page for that part.

 

 

 

Clearing the filter

Clearing the filter(s) will remove any of the search filters that you've applied. Select Clear Filter (1) will clear all filters that have been applied. Select the X for an applied filter (2) will clear that filter.
