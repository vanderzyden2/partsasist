Welcome to PartsASIST

PartsASIST is a revolutionary new e-commerce tool for end-user customers of our Volvo Trucks dealer network. With readily searchable, fully-integrated parts catalogs, it's easy to find and order parts from your local dealer.

Here is what PartsASIST offers you:

•Real-time inventory gives you confidence that you'll get your parts quickly and have your vehicles back up and running in no time.

•Skip the hold time by using our integrated VIN catalog. Find the part you need by vehicle and price, and then place your order in a single convenient experience.

•Use the All-Makes catalog to quickly find and order common parts.

•Intuitive search features provide several options to help you quickly find the right part.

•The Saved Orders feature gives you the option to immediately save your most frequently ordered parts.

•Be even more efficient by uploading an Excel file that contains your parts orders.

•Exclusive savings – take advantage of online-only promotions.

•24-hour parts counter – There's no need to wait for Parts department to be open to place orders

Gaining access to PartsASIST

There are two steps necessary to gain access to PartsASIST:

 

Step 1 – If you own Volvo Trucks, you'll need a Trucks Customer Portal and Fleet Impact access ID. For more information and requesting access, contact your local Volvo dealer.

 

If you are an independent truck repair shop, or non-Volvo fleet, you'll need access to the Trucks Customer Portal. For more information and requesting access, contact your local Volvo dealer.

 

If you have a current ID and password, proceed to step 2.

 

Step 2 – To view part pricing and availability and also place orders, your local Volvo Truck dealer must invite you to join their PartsASIST store. Contact your local dealer to have them verify that you have a registered account. They will send you an email invitation with a registration link to join.

 

Login to PartsASIST

After completing the steps above, you can access PartsASIST through www.volvotrucks.us with your ID and password. Log in using the Customer Login link at the top of the page. Or, select the Parts & Service tab to locate the PartsASIST link.

 

Maintain your VIN list

One of the key features in PartsASIST is the ability to assign a unit number or name to a specific VIN, which can be done using the Manage My Fleet feature in the Customer Services menu. To use this feature effectively, it will be important to accurately maintain the list of registered Volvo Truck VINs. If this list is not kept current, any new trucks that you acquire won't appear in PartsASIST. To update your VIN list, simply log in to the Volvo Trucks Customer Portal and complete the Fleet Impact Maintenance Form.

 

 

Frequently Asked Questions

VIN List

Why can’t I see all of my fleet VINs in my list?

We currently only maintain build specs for Volvo Trucks, so we are unable to fully support other makes at this time. However, we do recommend using our All-Makes catalog to search for a variety of product that will fit all other makes.

 

Also, keep in mind that we maintain a robust VIN database that supports vehicles dating back to 2010 for Volvo. Older models may not be visible in PartsASIST. Use your Fleet Impact tool to view these.

 

 

Catalogs and Parts

Why is the Genuine Catalog not visible?

If you do not have a registered Volvo VIN, the Genuine Catalog won't be visible.

 

How do I purchase parts from PartsASIST?

Once the dealer authorizes your account, you can login and begin to shop using the search features or any of our integrated catalogs.

 

What happens if my dealer has no availability for the parts I need?

Current dealership inventory is viewable within PartsASIST. However, a back order will be created if the part is priced at the dealership.

 

 

Purchasing

I currently purchase parts from a dealership and have established pricing levels. Will my pricing be reflected in PartsASIST?

Yes.

 

Can PartsASIST be set up to require a purchase order before to completing an order?

Yes.

 

 

General User

Can one user order for multiple Fleet locations?

Yes, but you will need to be invited by each dealership location from which you want to place orders.

 

Does PartsASIST support languages other than English?

Yes. The language preference can be made by PartsASIST users by Customer Services > Your Profile and selecting the correct language setting. Learn more in Your Profile.

 

If I'm a Fleet Manager, can I authorize additional users?

Yes, you can add user that can log in to your account to order for your company.
