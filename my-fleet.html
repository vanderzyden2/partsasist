My Fleet

From the Products menu, you can select the My Fleet option. This will display VINs that you have marked as favorites that have been set up in Manage My Fleet. If no favorites have been marked–and your fleet has less than 50 Mack or Volvo trucks–then all of your VINs will appear. If your fleet has more than 50 Mack or Volvo trucks, no VINs will appear but you will see the message Click here to Manage My Fleet.

 

Though 50 is the default number for Auto Populate My fleet, you can change this value in the Customer Services tab of Company Information.

 

NOTE: If this is your first time using PartsASIST, nothing may appear in the My Fleet listing. This is because you need to designate at least one favorite in Manage My Fleet.
