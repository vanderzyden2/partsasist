Promotions / Shop Special Offers

Shop Special Offers is a catalog that displays a listing of parts that are being offered with a promotional discount. The parts offered may consist of a specific category or a specific list of parts. The promotional price may be shown in the catalog, or a promo code will be given that will need to be applied at Check out page. The discount may be either a percentage or a dollar amount. Shop Special Offers may include parts that Mack Trucks (Volvo Trucks NA) is promoting and also parts that your local dealer is promoting. This catalog is accessible from the home page by clicking on the large Special Offers icon.

 

 

 

The Special Offers catalog is also available by clicking Products > Shop Special Offers in the menus,

 

 

 

Once you have navigated to the Special Offers Catalog, you will be presented with a listing of the available promotions categories.

 

 

 

Much like the way it's done in the Genuine Parts and Explore All Makes catalogs, simply click on any of the category links to display the list of parts for a specific promotion catalog. The listing includes the part number, description, stock status, price, and promotion details such as the discount amount and promotion code. From here, you can select the part and click the Add to Cart button to add it to the cart.

 

 

 

After adding the part to the cart, you can proceed to the Check out page. On the Checkout page, in the Promo Code field, enter the promotion code for that part, and click Add button to view the details of the promotion (adjacent to the field). The discount from the regular price will either be given as a percentage or a dollar amount. This total will reflect this discount.
