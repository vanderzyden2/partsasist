Home Page

The home page is the main page for the PartsASIST web store, and is the first page that appears when the clicking the PartsASIST icon on the Trucks Customer Portal. Refer to the figure below to see the location of each of these features:

 

1.Language – The language of the text throughout all catalogs.

2.Dealer – This is the dealership that will receive your orders. If you have an assignment to more than one dealer, choose one of the dealers from this drop-down menu.

3.User – Identifies the user is presently logged in to PartsASIST.

4.Full dealer name – The complete name of the dealership from which you are purchasing parts.

5.Navigation bar – There are three menu options on the Navigation bar: Products, Orders, and Customer Services.

6.Search field – Enter in a description or part number to begin a search.

7.Mini cart – Access to your shopping cart and displays current number of line items in your cart.

8.Catalog icons – These large icons are links to the catalogs that are accessible to your user profile.

9.Content area – There are seven different content pages. Click one of these large icons to see the destination page, which may be a PDF or a separate website.

10.Footer – At the very bottom of the page, you'll find links for contacting the local dealer, the terms and conditions, and terms of use.
