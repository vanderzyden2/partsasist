Explore All Makes

While the Genuine Catalog is specific to the Mack and/or Volvo trucks you have in your fleet, the All Makes Catalog contains parts information for those trucks and also parts that can fit other OEM brands.

 

To access the All Makes catalog, you can select it from the Shop All Makes Catalog icon on the home page:

 

 

 

Optionally, you can choose Products > ALL MAKES from the menu:

 

 

 

Let's work through an example search for an alternator.

 

NOTE: Your results are likely to differ from what appears in the figure, and will correspond to the vehicles in your fleet and the filters you apply.

 

 

Follow the steps below:

 

Step 1 – From the home page, choose the Explore All Makes Parts icon (see the figure above).

 

Step 2 – If necessary, scroll down and click the Rotating Electrical category (the icon that displays the two components).

 

 

 

Step 3 – Click the Alternators icon.

 

 

 

A listing will appear (similar to the figure below) that contains all alternators available for purchase from your dealer. For each item in the listing, you'll see the part number, description, application, size information–and also stock availability and pricing. To see this information, use the scroll bar at the bottom of the list.

 

 

To reduce the length of the list and focus on parts that are of interest to you, use any or all of the Attribute Filters on the lower left-hand side of the page.

 

If, for example, you only want to see the J180 Hinge mount offerings, simply check that box.

 

 

The list will decrease in size (likely fewer pages in the list) to display only the alternators that meet that criteria.

 

 

 

If necessary, click the buttons at the bottom of the list to view other pages in the list. When you locate a part of interest, click the blue-link part number to view the detail page for that part (it's a link to the catalog page).
