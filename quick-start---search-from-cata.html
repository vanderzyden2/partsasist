Quick Start - Search from Catalog Icons

Another way to search is to begin from the main page and click one of the large icons. You can choose from categories and subcategories, and then use filters to narrow the search results until you find the part you need. Throughout this tutorial, you'll find references to other topics that provide more details.

 

Example: Search for a brake drum in All Makes

Let's look at an example of how to search for a brake drum, beginning from the Catalog Icons on the main page.

 

Follow the steps below:

 

Step 1 – From the main page, choose one of the Explore All-Makes icon.

 

 

 

Step 2 – Click the Brakes icon.

 

 

 

Then, in the main panel, click Drums.

 

 

 

On the Drums page, you'll see a listing of all the brake drums that are available to order from the dealer.

 

 

To reduce the list down to see the brake drum that you want, scroll down to use the attribute filters in the bottom-left side of the page.

 

 

This will filter the results to the brake drums that fit those attributes, similar to what is shown in the figure below.

 

NOTE: Your results are likely to differ from what appears in the figure, and will correspond to the vehicles in your fleet and the filters you apply.

 

 

 

 

Step 3 – You may see additional pages in the search result. For example, you can click the number 3 to view the third page of the parts listing (step 1 in the figure below). Click on a blue link in the Part No column to display a detail page for that drum brake. For example, click on the GUN3922X part (step 2 in the figure below).  You'll see the part number, description, dealer stock status, and your customer specific pricing.
