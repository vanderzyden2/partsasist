Order Information

On this page, you can review any orders placed in PartsASIST. The Order Information page will only display RFQ and orders that were placed in PartsASIST. At the top of the page under Order Information, you can search for any RFQ or order by using the search box, or selecting a date range.

 

 

 

These are the categories to choose from:

 

•RFQ - Request For Quote

•Orders In Progress

•Order History

 

 

RFQ

RFQs in progress – These are all the RFQs that you've submitted. It shows the RFQ number, the date it was submitted, who submitted the RFQ, the reason the RFQ was generated, the status, how many items are on the RFQ, and the total current cost of the RFQ. Click the RFQ number to display the detail page of the RFQ. Learn more in Request For Quote.

 

 

 

Orders In Progress

Quotations to act on – Scroll down to view the quotes that have been sent back from the dealer for you to review. Click on the order number to see the order page. From there, you can add those parts to a Quick Order and proceed to the check out page.

 

 

Quotes returned to dealer – Scroll down to view the quotes that you have declined and sent back to the dealer for additional action. You may also decline a quote and close the RFQ. These will then be sent to the order history.

 

 

Active Orders – Scroll down to view the orders that have been sent to the dealer and are in the process of being finalized.

 

 

 

 

Order History

Scroll down to the very bottom to view all of the past orders that have been placed through PartsASIST and fully processed. Click on the order number to display the detail page. From there, you can add it to the Quick Order page and place the same order again.

 

 

You can also do the following:

 

•In the RFQ Number column, click an order link to view details for an order.
 

 

•On the detail page, click the Add to Quick Order button to add the same order to the Cart and Check out.
